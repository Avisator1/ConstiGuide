Constitution Info

The U.S. Constitution is an illuminating piece of work.

It stands as the cornerstone of liberty, justice, and equality in the United States.

Currently, the Constitution we know today is housed in the National Archives Museum.

Our Constitution is divided into seven articles and a preamble.

You may not have heard much about the preamble, but it serves as the introductory statement of the Constitution.

The preamble contains the iconic words, 'We the people.' This text, the preamble, essentially outlines the goals and purposes of the Constitution.
